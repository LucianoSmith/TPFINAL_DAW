Smart Home Help

New device:

To create a new device, simply click the (+) button or select "New Device" from the menu and then fill all the required information. When done, just hit "Save".

Device list:

This option will display information for all your devices. To edit, delete, and change the status of a specific device, simply click the "View" button.

View device information:

You can view device information just clicking over the circle in your house map or by clicking "View" button from the device list. From this window you can change the status of your device by modifying the switch or moving the slider.

Edit device information:

To edit the device information, go to the View window and then click the "Edit" button. Edit the information and finally click "Save".

Delete a device:

To delete a device, go to the View window and click the "Delete" button.
